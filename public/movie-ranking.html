The Most Impactful Movies Ever Made

My Top Three Favourites

Interstellar

This movie touched my heart like no other. A journey through space and time, a powerful depiction of the love and emotions between a father and his daughter transcending the barriers set by scientific theories, of black holes and beyond.

Kimi No Na Wa

The beautiful animation and unique storyline provided a pleasant visual and emotional experience. As different timelines collide with a profound purpose, Taki and Mitsuha must strive towards accomplishing the mission they didn't realise, they have been entrusted with.

San Andreas

A fun and thrilling watch, this movie is about the power of family and friends and what it takes to survive in the face of adversity. As earthquakes of magnitude 9 and above start hitting San Francisco, our main cast must find the courage to be resilient and overcome the overwhelming obstacles.
